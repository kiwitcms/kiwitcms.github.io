Kiwi TCMS conference presence, AW2019

11 events, 5 countries during Aug-Nov

Posted by Kiwi TCMS Team on Fri 09 August 2019 under community , events

Your favorite open source test case management system is going on tour again. During the next several months we will be at:

  • DEV.BG All in One, Aug 24, Sofia where we will have an info booth
  • Tabara de Testare Meetup, Sept 25, Bucharest where Alex Todorov will join a discussion panel. To be confirmed!
  • SEETEST, Sept 26-27, Bucharest - where you can meet with Alex Todorov and some of our community friends and contributors
  • RuseConf, Sept 28, Ruse - where Alex Todorov and our friend Gjore Zaharchev (Seavus, Macedonia) will host 2 presentations
  • Testival Meetup, Oct 1-2, Zagreb
  • PyCon Balkan, Oct 3-5, Belgrade where:
    • Alex Todorov will present Extending your static analysis for fun and profit
    • Kiwi TCMS team will host open source prints for 1 or 2 days (more info soon)
  • Quality Assurance According to Einstein, Oct 6, Kikinda where Alex Todorov will be meeting up with the local technical community as part of our joint work with Zamphyr
  • HackConf, Oct 11-13, Sofia where:
    • Rayna Stankova & Ivaylo Ivanov will be hosting Git for beginners workshop
    • Anton Sankov & Alex Todorov will be hosting Python for beginners workshop
    • you can use KIWITCMS discount code
  • TestCon Europe, Oct 15-17, Vilnius where Alex Todorov will present Finding security issues in open source and Extend your static analysis for fun and profit (backup presentation in case other speakers don't show up)
  • TestWarez, Oct 24-25, Ossa (near Warsaw).
  • OpenFest, Nov 2-3, Sofia where we will have an info booth
  • ISTA Conference, Nov 14-15, Sofia where you can meet with Alex Todorov and some of our contributors and customers

Feel free to ping us at @KiwiTCMS or look for the kiwi bird logo and come to say hi. Happy testing!
