Kiwi TCMS is going to FOSDEM 2019

Posted by Kiwi TCMS Team on Thu 17 January 2019 under community , events

Hello testers, Kiwi TCMS is going to FOSDEM this year. This is where you can find us:

  • Fri Feb 1st: after 18:00 @ Delirium Caf√© - we are taking part of the FOSDEM Beer Event where all participants are invited. Shout out with #KiwiTCMS on Twitter if you can't find us in the crowd
  • Both days: Building K, Level 2, Stand 10. We will be together with our friends from ReportPortal and SystemTestPortal at the Open Source Test Management stand
  • Sat Feb 2nd: 18:00 @ Room H.2215 (Lightning Talks) - 10 years of open source test case management with Kiwi TCMS lightning talk
  • Sun Feb 3rd: 10:00 @ UD2.120 (Python devroom) - How to write pylint plugins presentation with examples of plugins which our team uses to make Kiwi TCMS better

We would like to meet with all of you and talk about test management and test process organization. In case you are stuck for crazy ideas checkout our project mission for inspiration.
