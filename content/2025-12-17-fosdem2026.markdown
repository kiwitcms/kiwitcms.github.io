Hello testers, we are happy to announce that
Kiwi TCMS is going to [FOSDEM 2026](http://fosdem.org/) in Brussels.
This is where you can find us:


* Saturday Jan 31st:
  [Kiwi TCMS stand](https://fosdem.org/2026/news/2025-11-16-accepted-stands/) -
  most likely in the K building however specific information will be made available
  closer to the event! Please follow <https://fosdem.org/2026/> for more news!
* Sunday Feb 1st:
  [Testing and Continuous Delivery devroom](https://fosdem.org/2026/schedule/track/testing-and-continuous-delivery/) -
  we are proud to be co-hosting this devroom again!

We would like to meet with all of you and talk about open source, software testing and test management!
